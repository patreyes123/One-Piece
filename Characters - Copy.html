Straw Hat Pirates

Monkey D. Luffy

Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. He dreams of becoming the Pirate King and has the power of the Gum-Gum Fruit.

Roronoa Zoro

The swordsman of the crew, known for his three-sword style. He aims to become the world's greatest swordsman.

Nami

The navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. She's a skilled cartographer and dreams of creating a map of the entire world.

Usopp

The crew's sniper and storyteller. He dreams of becoming a brave warrior of the sea like his father.

Sanji

The cook of the crew, famous for his culinary skills and martial arts. He dreams of finding the All Blue.

Tony Tony Chopper

The doctor of the crew. A reindeer who ate the Human-Human Fruit, allowing him to talk and transform.

Nico Robin

The archaeologist of the crew, seeking the true history of the world. She ate the Flower-Flower Fruit.

Franky

The shipwright of the crew. A cyborg with a dream of building the ship that will take the Pirate King to the end of the Grand Line.

Brook

The musician of the crew. A living skeleton who ate the Revive-Revive Fruit and came back to life after dying.

Jinbei

The helmsman of the crew. A fish-man and former Warlord of the Sea who practices Fish-Man Karate.
